Career Profile

Jennifer Bronstein is known for her work as a TV producer. She has worked on Bravo's The Singles Project, TLC's Buying Naked, Food Network's Beat Bobby Flay, Travel Channel's Mysteries at the Museum, Animal Planet's Monsters Inside Me, HLN's Tim Ferriss Experiment amongst others. Outside of work, she's also a stage director for off broadway theater productions.
